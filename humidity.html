Latitude vs. Humidity

There does not appear to be a strict relationship between Latitude and Humidity percentage. Despite this, the most humid regions appear to reside +/- 20 degrees from the equator, while countries in the northern hemisphere tend to have higher humidity levels. This may be attributed to the fact that there is more land mass in the northern hemisphere than the southern.
